📊 Core Mechanic

Reliability Score

Your show-up rate over a rolling 6-month window, visible to every game host. Earn +2 pts for on-time arrival, +1 pt for playing. Last-minute drops hurt double.

💰 Accountability

Deposit System

Hosts set a $2–$10 deposit at game creation. Held — not charged — until game day. Show up and it's refunded. Ghost and it's forfeited.

⚡ Auto-Fill

Smart Waitlist

Waitlists are ordered by reliability score, not sign-up time. When someone drops, the most dependable person waiting gets the spot first.

⚡ Enforcement

Strike System

3 unexcused no-shows = temporary ban. Strikes expire with 90 days of clean attendance. Hosts can flag disputes. Excused absences don't count.

🍺 Retention

Post-Game Bar Deals

After a game ends, the app surfaces partner bar deals nearby. First round on the bar. Bars get a built-in crowd. Players stay in the ecosystem.

🌦️ Smart Alerts

Weather-Aware Games

Outdoor games auto-flag rain 12h out. Host gets an alert to cancel or move. Deposits stay held until a call is made — no money moves unfairly.

WHY YOU CAN'T

01

Your Score Lives Here.
